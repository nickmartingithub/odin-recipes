Stromboli

The inside of a stromboli.

Stromboli is a little bit like a rolled up pizza, with varying ingredients depending on what kind you make

  • Dough
  • Salami
  • Pepperoni
  • Ham
  • Cheese

These are the steps you need to take to enjoy this food!

  1. Lay out the dough
  2. Put all your ingredients on top of the dough
  3. Roll the dough into a log that has all the ingredients inside of it
  4. Put the uncooked stromboli inside the oven blah blah
