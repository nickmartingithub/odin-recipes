Tacos

Four beautifully made tacos.

Tacos are a fairly simple food that requires you to put your favorite ingredients inside of either a soft or a hard shell.

  • Hard tacos shells
  • Ground beef
  • Lettuce
  • Salsa
  • Cheese

These are the steps you need to take to enjoy this food!

  1. First you need to have the ground beef cooked before you begin
  2. Once the ground beef is cooked, place the meat inside of your taco shell
  3. Next, add whatever your favorite ingredients might be in whatever order you would like
  4. Last thing to do is just simply dig in! Enjoy your fantastic food
